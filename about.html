About Me

Hi there! I'm Ciara Stafford, a passionate software engineer with a background as a Registered Behavior Technician (RBT) and now a dedicated single mom to a wonderful toddler. My journey into the world of technology has been a dynamic one, driven by my insatiable curiosity and love for problem-solving.

Professional Background

My career in technology began with a transition from behavioral therapy to software engineering. Prior to diving into coding, I worked as a Registered Behavior Technician, where I developed invaluable skills in empathy, patience, and understanding human behavior. This experience laid a strong foundation for my journey into the tech industry, where I've found a new outlet for my passion for helping others through technology solutions.

With over a year of experience in the fintech sector, I've had the privilege of working on cutting-edge projects that have a real impact on people's lives. My role has exposed me to a wide range of technologies and methodologies, allowing me to continuously expand my skill set and adapt to new challenges.

Journey into Tech
